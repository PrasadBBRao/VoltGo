# VoltGo Brand Guidelines

## Brand Summary
VoltGo is a premium EV charging platform designed to feel modern, calm, and dependable. The brand should feel like a startup product that is confident, minimal, and built for the future of mobility.

## Visual Direction
- Light theme
- Fresh green primary palette
- Clean and airy spacing
- Minimal iconography
- Strong typography with a premium feel

## Logo Principles
- Flat and minimal
- No gradients
- No 3D effects
- Simple and scalable
- Suitable for favicon and app uses

## Recommended Usage
- Use logo-light.svg on light surfaces
- Use logo-dark.svg on dark surfaces
- Use favicon.svg for browser favicon and compact app marks

## Tone of Voice
- Friendly
- Confident
- Modern
- Straightforward
